About Us

Fine food and fun!

David and Robyn Clark purchased the property in the fall of 2014. They were frequent visitors to the former restaurant (The Black Frog) and own a home in the area. They have always had a dream of owning a restaurant as they love to feed, entertain and enjoy time with family and friends.

David worked 40 years at Bath Iron Works. After retiring from BIW in 2014, he has continued his shipbuilding career as a Consultant while pursuing his dream of owning a restaurant. Robyn has worked 26 years in the banking industry in Finance and is a graduate of Robert Morris University with a Bachelor’s Degree in Finance. She shares David’s dream of owning a restaurant and is now committing 100% of her time in helping this dream come alive.

Both David and Robyn have completed the State Certified Course in Restaurant Food Safety (ServSafe), have a strong work ethic and a passion for entertainment and ensuring guests eat well and enjoy.
